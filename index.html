+ HOW to use GRAPH effectively and efficiently.

++ By dragging some area of the graph, the area will be zoomed in.
++ To zoom out, double click the graph.

++ If you want to see some lines in the graph, you can choose the graph by clicking the labels on rightside.
++ When the label is not bright and clear, the label is 'DE-selected'.
++ When the label is bright and claer, the label is 'SELECTED'.

++ When you touch the line or marker, you can see the specific data of the plot.
